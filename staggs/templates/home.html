Quality is the Difference


We deeply value our customers, many of whom are not just patrons but neighbors and friends. Each plant in our care holds a special place in our hearts, and we are committed to their long-term health and vitality even beyond our grounds. This commitment is what defines our belief that 'Quality is the Difference.' At Staggs, quality isn't just a motto; it's a guiding principle we uphold in everything we do. Our dedication extends beyond our doors as we work tirelessly to make the Upstate a greener, more beautiful community. Every flower and shrub leaving our store is a testament to this commitment. We strive to offer only the healthiest, most vibrant plants. While we can't guarantee they'll maintain their perfection once they leave our care, we provide you with the tools and knowledge to nurture them. This, we promise.
